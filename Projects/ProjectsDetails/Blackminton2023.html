BLACKMINTON 2023

As the creator and organizer of the first edition of a badminton event, I led the project management while handling:

  • Registrations, reaching the maximum capacity with 83 participants and 20 visitors.
  • Partnerships with several companies, including Sonomax and Augereau.
  • Press relations through the distribution of a press kit and two press releases, resulting in the publication of two articles in the regional daily press.
  • Event promotion on social media (Instagram and Facebook) and print materials (flyers and posters).
    • This first edition was a success, with an overall participant satisfaction rating of 4.7/5. In 2024, a new edition was organized.
