Unis Vert Marbourg

The “Unis Vert Marbourg” project was created in response to the city of Poitiers' “Vivons l'Europe en route pour Poitiers Marbourg” challenge, organized as part of the 60th anniversary of twinning between the city of Poitiers and the city of Marbourg. The principle of the challenge is to imagine and organize a trip to Marbourg symbolizing Franco-German friendship, using alternative means of transport to planes and cars.

We carried out this project as a team of three, along with two other students from ESA (Groupe AFC). In a few words, the project aimed to travel with a low carbon footprint through 8 cities in 8 days while participating in activities related to ecology and Franco-German friendship. This was the first edition of this challenge, and we became its ambassadors. The project helped establish a partnership between our school and the city of Poitiers. Now, the initiative is renewed every year, with students from different cohorts taking part in the challenge.

On a professional level, Unis Vert Marbourg was my first experience as a project manager. I was able to apply everything I was learning during my training. In a versatile role, I was responsible for managing various aspects, including: project and team coordination, scheduling key dates, handling administrative processes, organizing project meetings and drafting minutes, creating illustrations and some communication materials, writing summaries for potential partners, developing project tracking indicators (PERT, Gantt chart, dashboard), as well as budgeting and financial monitoring.
