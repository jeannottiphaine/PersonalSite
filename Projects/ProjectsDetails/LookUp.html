LOOKUP

As part of my 5th year at ISCOM, I had the opportunity to take part in my first team challenge dedicated to innovation. The objective was to create a business around emotions in just 5 days... and we won. Our group chose to tackle a current and growing problem, which particularly affects young people under 35: the feeling of loneliness. The figures are worrying:

  • ➡️ +600% increase in chronic loneliness among young people in 10 years, innovations.
  • ➡️ 62% of young adults are affected by this phenomenon.

Faced with this reality, and in a world where 60% of encounters take place via apps, we wanted to change the rules. So we invented “LOOKUP”, the first physical device inspired by dating apps, designed to encourage people to connect in real life, directly in bars.
